Matchako Lounge

Making Friends Along The Way

 

Matchako Lounge is an indonesian doujin Circle run by three geologically separated people dedicated to The Touhou Fandom. Arranged back on the spring of 2019, The circle seeks to spread the Love of Touhou to the people around the archipelago, By making creative works that includes and not limited to : Artworks, Comic Books, Literature, and Merchandises. 

 

For years, we have been learning to improve our creations from numerous Art groups in hope to improve and create a better content for everyone, ultimately making new friends throughout the process.
